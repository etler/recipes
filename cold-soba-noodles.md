# Cold Soba Noodles

* 1/3 cup dashi
* 2 tbsp light soy sauce
* 2 tbsp cup mirin
* 1/8 tsp sugar
* 2 oz soba
* green onion

1. Bring dashi, soy sauce, mirin, and sugar to a boil, remove from heat and cool.
1. Boil soba for 3-5 minutes, rinse and chill in ice water, serve with dipping sauce and toppings.
