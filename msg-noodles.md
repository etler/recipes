# MSG Noodles

* 3 oz dry alkaline noodles or ramen noodles (5 oz fresh)
* 3/4 tsp msg
* 1 tbsp chili oil
* 1 tbsp pickled mustard greens
* 1/2 tsp sugar
* 1 tsp soy sauce
* 1 tbsp scallions, chopped
* cucumber, julienned

1. Combine all ingredients
