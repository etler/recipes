# Japanese Salad Dressing

* 1/2 tsp miso
* 1 tsp vinegar
* 1 tsp mirin
* 1 tsp sugar
* 1/4 cup mayo
* orange juice
* dash sesame oil
* ginger, grated

1. Combine all ingredients and add mayo until desired consistency

## Salad ingredients

* iceberg lettuce
* carrots
* tomato
* cucumber
* radish
* avocado
* orange
* sesame seeds
