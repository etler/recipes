# Dill Salmon

* handful of dill
* 1/2 cup creme fraiche
* 1 clove garlic, crushed in mortar and pestle
* 1 tbsp lemon juice
* olive oil
* salt
* pepper

1. [Follow baked salmon recipe](./baked-salmon)
1. Apply dill sauce after baking, add extra dill on top to garnish
