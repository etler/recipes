# Cranberry Sauce

12 ounces fresh or frozen cranberries
1 cup brown sugar
1/2 cup water
1 orange, juiced
1 tsp orange zest
1/2 tsp cinnamon
1/4 tsp allspice
1/4 tsp salt
1/8 tsp nutmeg
1/8 tsp cloves
1 shot spiced rum

1. Combine in pot, bring to boil over medium heat. Stir until berries have popped, about 10 minutes
