# Egg Drop Soup

* 1 egg, whisked
* 2 cup broth
* 1 tsp corn starch
* 1 tsp soy sauce
* 1 thumb ginger, pureed or grated
* scallions, chopped

1. Combine 2 tbsp of broth with corn starch
1. Bring broth to a boil and add soy sauce and ginger and corn starch slurry
1. Slowly drizzle egg into boiling soup, top with scallions
