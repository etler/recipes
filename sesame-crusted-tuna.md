# Sesame Crusted Tuna

* 1 stick butter
* 2 tuna steak
* 1/2 cup salt
* 2 qt water
* leek, shallot or garlic
* black and white sesame seeds
* pepper

1. Brine tuna in brine solution of salt and water for 15 minutes
1. Dry tuna with paper towel, add pepper, dredge in sesame seeds
1. Heat butter to 140Â°F degrees, add aromatics, cook tuna for 5 minutes each side
